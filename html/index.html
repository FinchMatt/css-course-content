About Me

29yo male with Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering and a Minor in Mathematics from Northern Arizona University. Graduated Summa Cum Laude from NAU in 2014 and began working as a lathe operator for Honeywell Aerospace, where I primarily performed early-in-sequence hog out operations on small and medium gears and shafts. After a year on the lathe, I was hired as a Product Design Engineer I for Honeywell Aerospace Repair & Overhaul's Phoenix Sky Harbor location.
I immediately inherited tooling engineering responsibilities from a quick-to-retire 50-year Honeywell lifer. Although the on-boarding left a lot to desire, I was able to get up to speed and quickly surpass the productivity of the career engineer; I was able to streamline and outsource much of the design work in order to meet the high demand of the facility.
